Our Programmes

Explore what CAISH has to offer, from research mentorship to community events.

MARS

Mentorship for Alignment Researchers. A research programme pairing aspiring AI safety researchers with experienced mentors from organisations like MIRI and Redwood Research, featuring an in-person kick-off week in Cambridge.

CaMLAB

An intensive ML upskilling programme for building technical AI safety skills. Build GPT-2 from scratch, learn interpretability techniques, understand RLHF, and replicate key papers. Expressions of interest now open.

Alignment Desk

A structured writing programme for those with existing AI safety exposure who want to produce and publish serious work. Weekly meetings, accountability, and feedback.

Socials & HDWSA

We run regular socials and How Do We Solve Alignment, a technical speaker series where researchers present papers and ongoing work. Weekly at Meridian.
