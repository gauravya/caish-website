AI Workload Verification

A structured reading course on AI workload verification and assurance, the field working on mechanisms that would let labs and states make agreements about AI development and check that no one is secretly defecting. By the end, our hope is you will be able to place different verification papers and setups by the claim they check, the evidence they use, and the institution that would act on it, and to have a project sketch of your own.
