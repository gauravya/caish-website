About Us

Building the AI safety community in one of the world's leading academic centres.

Our Mission

Advanced AI systems are poised to transform society in ways we're only beginning to understand. Alongside their potential benefits, they bring serious risksâ€”including, in the worst case, the possibility of existential catastrophe.

Cambridge AI Safety Hub exists because we believe this challenge demands the best minds working on it, and Cambridge is uniquely positioned to cultivate them. We run programmes and events that introduce talented researchers to AI safety, help them develop technical and governance skills, and connect them with a community of people who take these risks seriously.

The field is young. New projects need to be started. Ambitious people need to get things done. Our job is to find those people and give them the support, knowledge, and community they need to build a career in this space.
