MARS Programme

Mentorship for Alignment Researchers

MARS is a research programme that pairs aspiring AI safety researchers with experienced mentors into teams. The programme has a kick-off week in Cambridge—accommodation and meals provided—where you'll meet your cohort, attend talks and workshops, and make substantial progress on your project. From there you and your team will carry out 8-12 weeks of remote research, culminating in publications.

How it works

Who we're looking for

We welcome students and professionals with backgrounds in technical fields (ML, CS, cybersecurity, mathematics, physics, biology) or governance-relevant disciplines (law, international relations, public policy, political science, economics). Some prior exposure to AI safety, whether through the Alignment Fellowship, Bluedot, or similar programs, is helpful. Some projects may require specific technical skills or research experience, so check individual mentor listings for details. If a project excites you, we'd love to see you apply.
